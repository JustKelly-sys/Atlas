## What Atlas is (context)
Atlas is a payroll operations suite for mid-market companies across six countries. Ships five AI automations. Playroll's own strength is compliance-first, and termination checklists vary dramatically by jurisdiction — this page shows why.

## What this page is
This is `/app/people/terminations` — Termination Checklist Bot (NP-20). When a termination is logged (from HRIS webhook), Gemini 2.5 Flash generates a jurisdiction-specific checklist with statutory deadlines: California 72-hour rule, SA BCEA next-payday, UK P45 obligations, Germany contractual notice, UAE end-of-service gratuity, Saudi WPS.

## Functional requirements
- Reads from Supabase `terminations`, `termination_checklist_items`, `employees`, `countries`.
- "Log termination" button in header opens a dialog: employee picker (active employees only), termination type radio (voluntary/involuntary/deceased), notice date, last working day. Submit hits `/api/terminations/create` → n8n webhook → Gemini generates jurisdiction-specific checklist → writes termination + items to Supabase.
- Clicking a termination in the list loads its checklist in the right panel.
- Clicking the status circle of a checklist item toggles done/pending (writes to Supabase).
- Evidence upload is stubbed (disabled button with `Evidence` label).
